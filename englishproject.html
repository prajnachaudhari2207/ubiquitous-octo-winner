ILA SACHANI

Who is Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani, a talented Kathiawar embroidery artist, overcame physical challenges to excel in her craft. She learned embroidery using her feet under the guidance of her mother and grandmother. Ila's skills were recognized at a state exhibition, leading to national recognition and awards.

What is so special about Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani was born without hands in Amreli, Gujarat, and was unable to use her hands in the usual way. Ila's mother and grandmother were skilled in Kathiawar embroidery, a traditional art form, and taught her to create designs with her feet. She is very good at Kathiawar embroidery and creates beautiful designs for cushions, bedcovers, pillows, and more. She does all her work with her feet, including cutting fruits and vegetables, combing her hair, and doing needlework.

What is the story of Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani is a young girl known for her exceptional talent in embroidery, which she does with her feet due to a physical disability. Her story is significant as it highlights her determination, creativity, and the power of dreams, inspiring others to overcome challenges
